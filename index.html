VIRAT KOHLI

GREATEST OF ALL TIME

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He has achieved numerous records in international cricket and has been the captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats. Kohli is known for his aggressive batting style, incredible consistency, and strong leadership skills. Virat Kohli has an impressive list of achievements in cricket. These are just a few highlights of Virat Kohli's illustrious career in cricket.
